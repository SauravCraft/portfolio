ABOUT

I'm a B.Tech student and aspiring AAA gameplay programmer with a deep passion for crafting the systems that make games feel alive. My focus sits at the intersection of C++ engineering, network replication, and gameplay architecture.

I believe the best gameplay systems are invisible — they just feel right. Whether it's a finely-tuned inventory with drag-and-drop UX, an AI NPC that adapts to the player's behavior, or a lag-compensated combat system running across continents — I obsess over the details that players never notice but always feel.

I'm particularly drawn to AI-driven NPC systems using Behavior Trees and EQS, and scalable multiplayer architecture built for high-performance games. Currently building toward my first AAA-quality portfolio project and open to collaborations.
